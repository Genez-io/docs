# Describe Your Brand

A precise brand description helps Genezio represent your brand correctly in AI conversations. It improves the quality of generated scenarios and ensures AI systems evaluate your brand in the right context.

When Genezio first generates a description for you, it summarizes what your brand does, the category it belongs to, and the type of customers it serves. You review this description, edit it where needed, and confirm it.

---

## What a Good Description Captures

* **Core positioning** — what your brand does, in clear and non-promotional language.
* **Category and use cases** — the space you compete in and the problems you solve.
* **Differentiators** — what sets you apart in comparisons.
* **Honest constraints** — price tier, region, and integrations.

---

## Set Your Brand Language

You can set a **brand language** for your brand. This language is used to generate reports and conversations, so the way your brand is described and measured matches the market you serve.
