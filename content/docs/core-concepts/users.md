# Users

In Genezio, **users** are the people who can access and work inside an account.

Users can be invited at:

* **account level** (access to all brands in the account)
* **brand level** (access only to selected brands)

This allows teams to control collaboration and visibility across multiple brands.

---

## Account-Level Users

Account-level users are invited to the full account workspace.

This is useful for team members who need broad visibility, such as:

* account owners
* operations leads
* central growth or analytics teams

---

## Brand-Level Users

Brand-level users are invited to one or more specific brands.

This is useful when access should be limited, for example:

* client stakeholders who should see only their brand
* agency specialists assigned to selected client brands
* internal teams responsible for one product line

---

## Why This Matters

User access design affects both governance and day-to-day execution.

Clear user assignment helps teams:

* prevent accidental exposure across brands
* keep ownership and accountability clear
* collaborate faster within the right scope
