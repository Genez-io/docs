# What KPIs Are We Measuring

In Genezio, two core KPIs are used to evaluate how your brand performs in AI-generated answers:

* AI Recommendations
* AI Visibility

These KPIs answer different questions and should be read together.

---

## AI Recommendations

**AI Recommendations** is a percentage.

It measures the percentage of **Recommender Agent conversations** where your brand was recommended.

These conversations are usually **multi-turn**, based on scenarios, where the user (the persona) is looking for a specific, usually single recommendation that matches their needs as described in the scenario.

AI Recommendations helps you understand whether your brand is being actively suggested when users ask AI assistants to recommend a solution.

---

## AI Visibility

**AI Visibility** is a percentage.

It measures the percentage of eligible conversations where your brand appears in AI-generated answers.

This includes cases where your brand is:

* mentioned
* listed in recommendations
* included in comparisons

AI Visibility gives a broader view of how often your brand is present in AI answers across relevant conversations.

---

## How These KPIs Work Together

Use both metrics together:

* **AI Recommendations** tells you how often AI systems explicitly recommend your brand in recommendation-focused conversations.
* **AI Visibility** tells you how often your brand appears overall in eligible conversations.

A brand may have high visibility but lower recommendation rate, or the opposite. Reading both KPIs together gives a more complete view of AI performance.
