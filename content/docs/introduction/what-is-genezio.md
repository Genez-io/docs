# What is Genezio?

Genezio is a platform that helps organizations understand and improve how their brand appears in answers generated by Large Language Models (LLMs) such as ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity.

As more users rely on AI assistants to research products, compare services, and make decisions, visibility inside AI-generated answers becomes an important part of a company's digital presence.

Genezio helps teams measure, analyze, and improve this presence.

## The Shift from Search Engines to AI Answers

Traditionally, users discovered brands through search engines such as Google.

The typical interaction looked like this:

1. A user enters a search query.
2. The search engine returns a list of links.
3. The user clicks one or more results.

In AI-powered search, the experience is different.

Instead of showing a list of links, the AI system generates a direct answer based on information retrieved from multiple sources.

Example:

> **User query:** ***What are the best running shoes for marathon training?***

Instead of displaying links, the AI assistant may produce an answer like:

> **AI answer:** The best running shoes for marathon training include the Nike Alphafly, Adidas Adios Pro, and Saucony Endorphin Elite due to their energy return and cushioning.

The sources used to generate this answer may include:

- product review sites
- ecommerce stores
- brand websites
- forums and communities
- editorial articles

In this environment, visibility is determined not only by search ranking, but by whether the AI system mentions or cites a brand inside its generated response.

## AI Visibility

Genezio focuses on measuring and improving AI Visibility.

AI Visibility refers to how frequently and how prominently a brand appears in answers generated by AI systems.

This includes:

- brand mentions
- citations of the brand's website
- comparisons with competitors
- sentiment associated with the brand

For example, if a user asks:

> **User query:** ***What is the best CRM for startups?***

An AI assistant might answer:

> **AI answer:** Popular CRM solutions for startups include HubSpot, Salesforce, and Pipedrive.

In this scenario:

- HubSpot, Salesforce, and Pipedrive have visibility.
- Any CRM not mentioned has no visibility in that answer.

Genezio helps companies measure this visibility across many questions and AI systems.

## How Genezio Works

At a high level, Genezio simulates real user interactions with AI systems and analyzes the responses.

The process includes several steps.

### 1. Define Topics

Topics represent the areas where a brand wants to appear in AI-generated answers.

Examples:

- "running shoes for marathon training"
- "best CRM for startups"
- "luxury ski resorts in Switzerland"

### 2. Create Scenarios

Scenarios represent realistic user prompts that someone might ask an AI assistant.

Example scenario:

> **User query:** ***I am training for my first marathon. What running shoes should I buy?***

### 3. Run Conversations

Genezio runs conversations with supported AI engines such as:

- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Gemini
- Perplexity

Each conversation simulates a real user asking questions and sometimes asking follow-up questions.

### 4. Analyze the Answers

The platform then analyzes each response to extract structured information, including:

- brands mentioned
- sources cited
- statements about each brand
- sentiment associated with those statements

### 5. Generate Insights

Using this data, Genezio calculates metrics and generates insights such as:

- AI Visibility score
- share of voice compared to competitors
- most cited sources
- opportunities for content improvements

## What Genezio Measures

Genezio analyzes several aspects of AI-generated answers.

### Brand Mentions

How often a brand appears in responses generated by AI assistants.

### Citations

Which sources the AI system references when generating answers.

These may include:

- brand websites
- review sites
- news articles
- blogs

### Competitor Presence

Which other brands appear in the same answers.

This helps identify competitors in the AI landscape.

### Sentiment

Whether statements about a brand are:

- positive
- neutral
- negative

### Share of Voice

The relative visibility of a brand compared to competitors across many AI conversations.

## Why This Matters

AI assistants are becoming a common starting point for product discovery and research.

Users increasingly ask questions such as:

- "What are the best running shoes?"
- "What CRM should a startup use?"
- "What is the best credit card for travel?"

The brands that appear in these answers gain exposure and influence over purchasing decisions.

Organizations that are not mentioned in these responses may miss opportunities to reach potential customers.

Genezio helps companies understand this new landscape and take actions to improve their visibility.

## What Genezio Does Not Do

Genezio does not control how AI systems generate answers.

Instead, it helps organizations:

- measure how their brand appears in AI responses
- understand why certain sources are cited
- identify opportunities to improve their visibility

Changes to visibility typically come from improving content, authority, and relevance across the web.
